Certification

At Infty, we believe in empowering professionals through certification. Our commitment to excellence drives us to offer top-notch certification programs that enhance your skills and open doors to new opportunities.

Why Choose Infty Certification?

  • Industry Recognition: Our certifications are recognized globally, giving you a competitive edge in the job market.
  • Quality Assurance: We adhere to best practices in certification development, ensuring validity, reliability, and fairness.
  • ISO Accreditation: Infty certifications align with ISO 9001 and ISO/ANSI 17024 standards, guaranteeing quality and credibility1.

Available Certifications

Project Management Professional (PMP)®

  • The PMP® certification is for project managers who want to demonstrate their expertise in leading successful projects.
  • Domains covered: People, Process, Business Environment.
  • Prepare with our comprehensive study materials and expert guidance.

Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM)®

  • Ideal for aspiring project managers or team members.
  • Covers foundational project management concepts.
  • Kickstart your career with CAPM® certification.

Certified Information Systems Auditor (CISA)®

  • For professionals in IT audit, control, and security.
  • Domains include Professional Conduct, Risk Management, and more.
  • Prove your competence with CISA® certification.

Certified ScrumMaster (CSM)®

  • Designed for individuals interested in agile project management.
  • Covers Scrum framework, roles, ceremonies, and artifacts.
  • Elevate your career with CSM® certification.

Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH)®

  • Tailored for cybersecurity professionals and ethical hackers.
  • Domains include network security, penetration testing, and vulnerability assessment.
  • Validate your skills with CEH® certification.

Certified Data Scientist (CDS)®

  • Geared towards data scientists and analysts.
  • Covers statistical modeling, machine learning, and data visualization.
  • Boost your credentials with CDS® certification.

Getting Certified: What You Need to Know

  • Eligibility: Check the prerequisites for each certification.
  • Preparation: Study the content outline and recommended resources.
  • Exam: Our exams assess real-world skills and knowledge.
